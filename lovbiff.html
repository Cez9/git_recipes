Ingredients

  • Thin beef steak slices
  • Beef seasoning
  • Whipping cream
  • Onions
  • Chili flakes
  • Garlic powder
  • Butter
  • Soy sauce

    • Steps

    1. Fry butter in a pan
    2. Begin frying the steak slices at a medium to high temperature
    3. Add salt & Pepper after preference
    4. Once the meat is cooked on both sides and in the middle, lower the heat
    5. Pour in about 1 decileter of whip cream in the pan
    6. Adjust the heat so that the cream is simmering
    7. Add chili flakes and garlic powder after preference
    8. Add soy sauce to give the it a darker color and more salty flavor
    9. Remove the meat from the pan but leave the cream sauce in
    10. Put as much onion as you would like in the pan
    11. Fry the onion
    12. Add sugar to the onion and let it simmer with the cream sauce
    13. Pour the cream sauce with glazed onion over the meat, if you want to re-heat the meat you can put it back in the pan for a bit.
